Bonfire Jam

A puzzle-ish platformer game made for the Spellkollektivet Bonfire Jam by ZigZagZat and Toffee1347. You must trade your soul with the devil to swap into the body of lost adventurers. You have a limited amount of souls each level, so be careful! Each one has a unique ability that helps you beat the levels. You cannot do it alone. It has short sequence of 8 levels due to time limitations, but may be expanded upon.

AD - Movement

Space - Jump

E - Interact

R - Restart Level
